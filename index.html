Our Story

Local Madison Barber Eddie Luna has worked hard to maintain and establish his very own business, Neat Fades Barbershop, here in his hometown of Madison, WI. Against the odds of opening during the COVID-19 pandemic in 2020, Neat Fades Barbershop is Eddie's first step in providing quality haircuts for the people in the local Madison community. Additionally, he is providing chances to up-and-coming barbers within the local area with opportunities for barbering growth and development.

Here at Neat Fades Barbershop, we are dedicated to making people feel confident (and crispy) while providing a home away from home here at the barbershop.
